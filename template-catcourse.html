Course Description

Learn the leadership and communication skills to support quality management in your organization. Through team-based projects, learn how to master the most difficult aspects of quality management. Discover how quality management ensures that concepts—such as teamwork, employee empowerment and continuous improvement—don't degenerate into buzzwords that increase cynicism and decrease employees responsiveness to organizational goals.
